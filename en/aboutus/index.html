What differentiates us

  • Goal

    Support energy network operators and related industries in digitization.

  • Know-How

    Interdisciplinary know-how from engineering and computer science combined with the technical possibilities of the cloud.

  • Founding

    Amperias was founded in 2019 by employees of a distribution network operator.

  • Philosophy

    We want to actively support network operators in digitization projects. In doing so, we combine the know-how of energy and information technology with practical experience at the network operator. As a link between the two worlds, we can purposefully drive digitalization in the energy industry.
