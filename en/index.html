We bring digitization to the network operators.

We advise on digital transformation and develop individual software solutions for our partners.

We offer know-how on different levels.

  • Softwaredevelopment

    We advise on digital transformation and develop individual software solutions for our partners. From the analysis of existing processes and the establishment of projects to the development and implementation of suitable software solutions. We take care of everything!

  • Cloud Engineering

    Our goal is the holistic development of digital and extensible toolboxes in the cloud without vendor lock-in. For this purpose, we identify and develop various components together with our partners* and take care of the orchestration in the cloud.

  • Research

    or new ideas and unconventional requirements, we offer our partners the initiation and support of application-oriented research projects on the topic of digitization. We also help them to identify public funding and to exploit the research results for their business development.

Together and digitally to the energy transition

amperias GmbH is a young company with the goal of supporting energy network operators and related industries in digitalization. For this purpose, we rely on interdisciplinary know-how from engineering and computer science in combination with the technical possibilities of the cloud.

More about us

The interdisciplinary nature of digitalization projects at network operators requires diverse skills from energy and information technology. That's why we combine know-how from engineering and information technology with practical experience at the network operator. As a link between the two worlds, we can purposefully drive digitalization in the energy industry.
